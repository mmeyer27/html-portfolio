TheBest Movies According to Meg

My top 3 movies of all time.

Star Wars: A New Hope

This is the one that started it all! So innovative and ahead of its time. Plus, a fun story, and great characters!

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Who doesn't love Indiana Jones? And Marion? And a wholloping great adventure?
This is one of the few movies that made me research the bible to read about the Ark of the Covenant.

The Sound of Music

Talk about classics! The story is wonderful. But the most important element is Julie Andrews! She is an amazing actress and singer! And all the child actors were also great!
